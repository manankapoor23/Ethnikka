About us

Welcome to Ethnikka, where tradition meets the future of fashion. We are a vibrant brand dedicated to redefining traditional Punjabi clothing for the modern girl. Our passion lies in honouring the rich cultural heritage of Punjab while seamlessly blending it with the dynamic and bold styles that resonate with Gen Z and Gen Alpha. At Ethnikka, we believe that fashion is a powerful expression of identity, and our collection is designed to empower young girls to celebrate their roots while confidently embracing contemporary trends. Each piece in our collection is a unique fusion of classic Punjabi silhouettes with innovative designs, vibrant colours, and playful patterns that reflect the spirit of today's youth.

Our Brands

Modrnfolks

MODRNFOLKs is a dynamic fashion brand that stands at the intersection of heritage and contemporary style. Specializing in curating collections that celebrate the diversity of global traditions while infusing them with modern, urban aesthetics. Our pieces are designed for those who appreciate the richness of cultural attire but seek to wear it with a fresh, forward-thinking twist.

UrbanRoots

UrbanRoots is a cutting-edge fashion brand dedicated to blending the essence of traditional craftsmanship with the pulse of contemporary urban life. UrbanRoots takes inspiration from the rich cultural heritages of the world and reimagines them for the modern wardrobe. Their collections are a celebration of authenticity and innovation, offering garments that seamlessly fit into the bustling rhythms of city life while staying true to their roots.
